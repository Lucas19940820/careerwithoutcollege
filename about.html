MISSION STATEMENT

Produce well-trained entry-level truck drivers who can transition into gainful employment in their respective fields upon completion of the vocational training objectives.

VISION

  • CW College aspires to be a vocational institution that serves at a level of national quality by maintaining our long-term trend-setting role within the trucking industries.
  • CW College strives to excel and exceed the industry standard for vocational training which will allow our graduates to achieve gainful employment.
  • CW College will continue to provide distinctive training programs for truck drivers.

    • CORE VALUES

  • Start and end with the student driver in mind
  • Maintain a high level of integrity and ethical values
  • Be accountable for our actions and deliver the right result the first time
  • Maintain diversity and cultural sensitivity towards all people
  • Practice mutual respect and civility
  • Be a good neighbor and provide a positive contribution to our community
