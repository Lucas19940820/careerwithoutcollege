Overview

Career without College Pty Ltd is a South African Company offering comprehensive road safety training for road users, especially truck drivers and those conveying dangerous goods by road. The company was established with the aim of promoting road safety and transport-based education.

CW College (Pty) Ltd is led by a team of professionals dedicated to improving road safety skills and competency levels for road users in South Africa and across our borders. We aim to become a leading local and national player in professional driver training and development, ensuring that our training modules are up-to-date and aligned with traffic laws and regulations.
