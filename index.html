Overview

Career without College Pty ltd is a South African Company offering comprehensive road safety training for road users especially truck drivers and those conveying dangerous goods by road. The company was set up with the sole mandate of promoting, training and development of transport based education which will help curb the gap in the South African economy which will be provincially and nationally competitive by providing facilities and material to educate the nation.

CW College (Pty) Ltd is led by a team of professionals that are dedicated to improving road safety skills and competency levels of all road users in South Africa and across our borders. This will be achieved through CW College becoming a leading local and National player in professional driver training and development, by being responsive to the road and traffic laws and regulation changes, and most importantly, by being able to translate the laws and regulations to training modules for the various targeted stakeholders.
